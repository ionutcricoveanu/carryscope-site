How it works

  1. Same data, honest math. We start from the same funding and basis rate the popular funding dashboards show you, then subtract the costs those dashboards leave out: trading fees and the basis half-spread.
  2. We compare it to risk-free. A carry below what you'd earn risk-free isn't worth the liquidation and operational risk.
  3. Coming for subscribers: risk-adjusted columns (Sharpe, max drawdown, a bear-market drawdown), a regime signal, alerts, and more symbols.
